Empowering Citizens Through Transparent Grievance Resolution

A digital platform that bridges the gap between citizens and government authorities.

This platform allows citizens to submit civic complaints related to public infrastructure such as roads, electricity, water supply, sanitation, and street lighting. Users can track complaint progress until resolution by authorities.

Benefits of the System

  • Faster complaint resolution
  • Transparent complaint tracking
  • Automatic department assignment
  • Improved communication between citizens and authorities
  • Data analytics for better governance

How the System Works

  1. Citizen registers and submits a complaint
  2. Complaint is automatically assigned to the responsible department
  3. Officers review and assign workers
  4. Worker resolves the issue
  5. Citizen receives updates and final resolution
