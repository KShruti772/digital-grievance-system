Empowering Citizens Through Transparent Grievance Resolution

A digital platform that bridges the gap between citizens and government authorities

This platform allows citizens to submit civic complaints related to public infrastructure such as roads, electricity, water supply, sanitation, and street lighting. The system ensures transparency by allowing users to track the progress of their complaints until they are resolved by authorities.

How It Works

Simple 5-step process for resolving your grievances

1

Register an Account

Create a free account as a citizen, officer, or worker. It takes just a minute to set up your profile and start using the platform.

2

Login to the System

Log in with your credentials to access your personalized dashboard where you can view all your activities and manage your account.

3

Submit a Complaint

Describe your grievance in detail. Include information about the issue, location, category, and attach relevant photos or documents for better documentation.

4

Track the Progress

Watch your complaint move through the system. See updates as it's assigned to officers and workers, and stays informed about every development.

5

Complaint Resolution

Once authorities resolve your issue, your complaint status is updated to "Resolved". Provide feedback to help us improve the system.

About This System

This project aims to improve communication between citizens and government departments by providing a centralized digital platform for grievance reporting and resolution.

Our mission is to create a transparent and efficient system where:

  • Citizens can easily report issues
  • Government stays accountable
  • Problems get resolved faster
  • Transparency is maintained
  • Citizens can track progress
