Empowering Citizens Through Transparent Grievance Resolution

The Digital Grievance Redressal System allows citizens to report civic issues such as damaged roads, water supply problems, electricity issues, sanitation problems, and more. The system enables authorities to manage complaints efficiently and ensures transparency in resolving public grievances.

Submit Complaint Login

Benefits of the System

Easy Complaint Submission

Citizens can easily report issues through an online complaint form without any hassle or complex procedures.

Real-Time Status Tracking

Users can track the progress of their complaints in real-time and stay updated on every action.

Faster Problem Resolution

Authorities can assign workers and resolve issues quickly through efficient workflow management.

Transparent Governance

Ensures accountability and efficient grievance management with complete transparency and record-keeping.

How to Use the System

1
Register an Account

Create a free account by providing your basic details and email address.

2
Login to the System

Log in with your credentials to access the complaint submission dashboard.

3
Submit a Complaint

Fill out the complaint form with details, location, category, and attach photos if needed.

4
Authorities Review & Assign

Authorities review your complaint and assign field workers to resolve the issue.

5
Issue Resolved

Once resolved, you'll receive a notification and can view the completion details.

About the Project

The Digital Grievance Redressal System is designed to improve communication between citizens and government authorities by providing a digital platform to report and resolve civic issues efficiently.

This innovative system bridges the gap between citizens and government departments, ensuring that every civic complaint is tracked, managed, and resolved with complete transparency and accountability.
